(8) Maximum building coverage. No more than 20% of the area of any lot, as
measured in the first 120 feet of lot depth, shall be covered by above-grade
buildings and/or structures. Floodplains, detention basins, decks and patios, as
defined herein, shall not be considered as a building or structure for purposes
of computing this coverage.
(9) Maximum improvement coverage. No more than 35% of the area of any lot
shall be covered by physical improvements, including but not limited to any
above-grade buildings and/or structures, as well as at-grade structures,
including but not limited to ingress/egress platforms, sidewalks, parking areas,
patios, driveways, swimming pools, etc.; but including aboveground decks. It
is the intention of this provision that each lot shall have at least 65% of its lot
area without any improvements except vegetation. Flood detention basins as
defined herein shall not be considered as a building, structure, or improvement
for purposes of computing this coverage.

R-150 Zone District must comply with all applicable regulations of this chapter.
